FAQ

Which mechanical switch is best for coding?

Tactile switches (Cherry MX Brown, Gateron Jupiter Banana) offer the best balance — feedback without noise. Heavy linear switches work well too if you prefer smooth keystrokes.

Are mechanical keyboards worth it for developers?

Yes. A good mechanical keyboard lasts 10+ years. At $150-200, that's $15-20/year for a tool you use 8+ hours daily.

What's hot-swappable?

Hot-swappable keyboards let you change switches without soldering. Essential for experimenting with different switch types.
