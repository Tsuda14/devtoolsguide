## FAQ

### Which mechanical switch is best for coding?
Tactile switches (Cherry MX Brown, Gateron Jupiter Banana) offer the best balance — feedback without noise. Heavy linear switches (MX Black, Gateron Yellow) work well too if you prefer smooth keystrokes.

### Are mechanical keyboards worth it for developers?
Yes. A good mechanical keyboard lasts 10+ years. At $150-200, that's $15-20/year for a tool you use 8+ hours daily. The typing comfort and accuracy improvement is immediately noticeable.

### What's the difference between hot-swappable and soldered?
Hot-swappable keyboards let you change switches without soldering. Essential if you want to experiment with different switch types.
